Privacy Policy for Collections Extension

Last Updated: February 2025

Overview

Collections Extension is an open-source browser extension that helps users organize their browser tabs and pages. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Storage

We do not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All your collection data is stored locally on your device using browser storage APIs.

Google Drive Integration

  • We use Google OAuth for syncing your collections to your Google Drive
  • We only request access to:
    • Create/modify its own application data in your Google Drive
    • View and manage its own configuration data in your Google Drive
  • Access tokens and refresh tokens are stored only on your local device
  • Token refresh operations are processed through our Cloudflare Worker solely for secure client secret management

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (through extension settings)
  • Disable Google Drive sync at any time
  • Revoke Google OAuth permissions

Cookie Usage

This extension does not use cookies. We use browser's local storage API to store your preferences and collections.

Browser Permissions

The extension requires these permissions for functionality:

  • Storage: To save your collections locally
  • Tabs & ActiveTab: To manage and interact with browser tabs
  • Identity: For Google OAuth authentication
  • SidePanel: For extension interface

Third-Party Services

We do not share any data with third parties. The only external service used is Google Drive API for syncing your collections, which is completely optional.

Data Security

All your data is stored either locally on your device or in your personal Google Drive account if you choose to enable sync. We never have access to your data.

Changes to Policy

Any changes to this privacy policy will be updated on this page and in our GitHub repository.
